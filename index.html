I'm Tiyana Waldron

Media Production and Theatre Student

Bio

Tiyana Waldron is a passionate performer and storyteller with experience in theatre, film, and directing. She has recently started her second year at Howard Community College, where she is studying audio video media production and theatre. Tiyana has appeared in a range of classical and contemporary stage productions like Eurydice and Twelfth Night. Expanding her creative work beyond the stage, she wrote, directed, and starred in an independent short film entitled Left Behind and served as director and cinematographer for a fan-made music video to AnnenMayKantereit and Giant Rooks’ rendition of Tom’s Diner.

In recognition of her talent and dedication, Tiyana received the Director’s Award for Theatre in high school. Alongside her academic and artistic pursuits, she works as a hostess at First Watch, where she loves helping and bringing joy to others. In her free time, she loves creating characters and backstories for Dungeons & Dragons, blending her passion for performance with imaginative worldbuilding. Her ultimate goal is to use her love of acting and theatrical performance to craft films that feel more alive, dynamic, and emotionally engaging for audiences.
